About

Breathing New Life into
Cherished Pieces

Welcome to my world of upcycled furniture. I'm Ellie, and I believe every piece of furniture has a story waiting to be told. Based in the beautiful village of Lymm, Cheshire, I specialise in transforming preloved furniture into stunning, bespoke pieces that blend timeless elegance with sustainable living.

Each creation is lovingly restored by hand, using premium paints and finishes to ensure lasting beauty. Whether you're looking for a statement piece for your home or wish to give new life to a treasured heirloom, I'm here to help you create something truly special.
